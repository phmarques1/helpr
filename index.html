Introducing Helpr

Your on demand assistant for household chores & services

Be less busy

Helpr is a free, messaging-based, on demand service that takes the pain out of household activities. From delivering your groceries to having a pumbler fix your kitchen sink drain, we’ve got you covered. Make a request by instant messaging and consider it done. Hassle-free

We'll clean your home

So... your home is a mess and you’re too busy to clean it yourself? We’ll find you a top rated cleaner and send him/her to make your home spotless again.

We'll buy & deliver your groceries

We know how painful it can be to go to the nearest market just to buy the missing ingredients to that super fancy dinner you promised to cook for friends and family. How about those long lines at the checkout? Yea. It sucks. That’s why we’ll do it for you.

Helpr is 100% free

Yes, it’s free. No hidden fees, no markups. Nothing. When you place a request with us, you’ll pay the same amount as if you had requested it yourself. We’re here to make your life easier.

We'll do your laundry

We bet there are more interesting things to do other than your laundry, right? Yeah, we thought so. Spend your time doing the things you love. Leave the boring stuff to us.

We’ll find you a painter, a pumbler and more…

Finding a good home service professional can be very time consuming and frustrating. We’re on a mission to simplifying this process by making it as easy as sending a text message.

Simple, integrated checkout

Once you’ve asked for what you need, check if everything is correct and confirm your request on the fly. It really is as simple as that!

Reclaim your life

Less household chores. More quality time with friends and family. Our helprs are on call to help you with what you need when you need it.
